Our Story

Who We Are

Whether you're printing functional stoneware on a Cerambot, firing porcelain from a Delta-style clay printer, or running Lithoz LCM systems for dental zirconia — this is your community.

We cover the full spectrum of ceramic additive manufacturing: machine comparisons, clay body formulation tips, firing schedule guides, and links to the latest peer-reviewed research.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the most authoritative specialist resource for 3d print ceramics — connecting researchers, designers, and manufacturers with expert coverage and fabrication services.

Our Vision

A world where every ceramic form — however complex — can be produced additively with precision and speed.
